Frequently Asked Questions

What is the security PIN used for?

Your 4-digit security PIN is required to authorize transactions, especially withdrawals. This adds an extra layer of security to protect your funds.

Why is there a $500 network fee for withdrawals?

The network fee covers transaction processing, security verification, and banking infrastructure costs. This ensures your funds are transferred securely and efficiently to your designated bank account.

How long does it take to receive withdrawn funds?

Withdrawals are typically processed within 2-3 Minutes. Processing are always fast.

Is my personal and banking information secure?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption and security protocols to protect your data. We never share your information with third parties without your consent.
